Talent Acquisition — Tech / Software Company

TA teams at software companies face a double disruption: AI is automating their own workflows and fundamentally changing the roles they hire for. Entry-level tech hiring at the top 15 firms fell 25% from 2023–2024 and continued into 2026 — directly compressing TA volume and reshaping recruiter priorities.

⚠️ Software company context: In a software company, TA teams must also deeply understand the roles they hire for — AI engineering, LLM pipelines, agentic workflows. As junior tech roles shrink (Stanford 2026: -20% for devs aged 22–25), recruiters are hiring for more senior, specialised profiles. The 'AI boomerang' trend (Robert Half, April 2026: 29% of companies rehire after AI layoffs) also shows that TA capacity planning is harder than ever.
The TA role is splitting in two
AI-automated sourcerHigh risk
Handles volume: sources, screens, schedules, sends outreach. Largely agentic AI by 2027 (Gartner). SHRM 2026: AI now in 43% of TA workflows.
Talent strategist & advisorResilient
Consults hiring managers, builds relationships, drives employer brand, closes senior talent. Irreplaceable.
